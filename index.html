Summary of Findings

Based on the analysis of the Zomato dataset, we observed the following trends:

  1. Restaurant Types:

    • Dining restaurants receive the highest number of votes (20,363), significantly outperforming other categories.
    • Cafes and Buffets have lower engagement compared to dining places.

  2. Cost for Two People:

    • The most common cost for two people is 300, followed by 200 and 150.
    • Mid-range options (400-800) are also prevalent, indicating a mix of affordable and moderately priced eateries.

  3. Online Orders:

    • A majority of the restaurants listed do not accept online orders (90 vs 58).
    • However, the analysis suggests that restaurants accepting online orders might have a different rating distribution, which is further explored in the visualizations.

  4. Ratings:

    • The ratings vary across different types of restaurants and costs, with specific clusters visible in the visualizations.
